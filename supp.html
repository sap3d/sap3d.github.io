2. Novel View Synthesis

We present generated novel views for a varying number (1 to 5) of input images to SAP3D.

For each input set, we provide the novel views before and after the fine-tuning stage. We use stochastic conditioning in all the experiments.

Similarily to 3D reconstruction, with more input images, the novel view synthesis of SAP3D improves. Moreover, the adapation stage improves the consistency of the generations.
